abstract: In this extended abstract, we present ongoing work on a novel functional
  representation of persistence diagrams (PDs). Building on the approach of \citet{gotovac2025topological},

  of finite integrable measures. Unlike their method, however, our construction yields
  a bi-continuous operator that is stable with respect to the 1-Wasserstein distance.
